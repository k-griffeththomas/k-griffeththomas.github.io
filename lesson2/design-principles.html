Design Principles Applied

Mobile Views Made Excellent

Visual Hierarchy

Wonder Bread

wonderbread.com

Wonder Bread is a classic brand that knows what they want their customers to do - feed themselves with Wonder Bread! This is apparent through the largest font saying "Feed Your Joy" and the secondary element being the brand logo. The site is simple, but very strategic in how they move the viewers eye over the page using Visual Hierarchy in an effort to lead them where they want them to end up.

Rule of Thirds

Joshua Cripp Photography

joshuacripps.com

Photographers have the Rule of Thirds top of mind in all work they are doing since it is a solid design principle, so of course Joshua Cripp Photography's site shows a perfect example of the rule of thirds in the images placed on his site. Both images use the Rule of Thirds, by placing the focal points of each photograph at both the intersection and the lines of the 3x3 grid which gives a professional feel and design to this site.

White Space

Soul Honey Clothing

soulhoneyclothing.com

This is one of my favorite sites. It is so simple and clean. Navigating it is a breeze since they have wisely eliminated any potential distractions with their excellent use of white space. Soul Honey Clothing allows the viewer to focus on the few elements of the site and the actions the company wants them to take.
